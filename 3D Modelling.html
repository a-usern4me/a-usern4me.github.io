3D Environment/Habitat

Blender - December 2023

Coursework utilising photorealistic 3D renders of a living space to make inferences about the inhabitants. This approach was a futuristic mobile home used for traversal and scavenging by desert nomads, combining Mars rovers, mecha and Bedouin culture. The large tank treads are wrapped using an array modifier.
