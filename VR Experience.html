Immersive VR Experience

Unity - April 2025

Tasked with creating an immersive VR experience with a renewable energy theme, with the user carrying out various maintenance tasks on an oil rig to repel a Kaiju-esque monster. Used immersive design elements e.g. environmental effects such as heavy rain and audio/visual cues to craft atmosphere and utilised HCI design concepts such as affordances to communicate tasks and the uses of interactable objects to the player.
